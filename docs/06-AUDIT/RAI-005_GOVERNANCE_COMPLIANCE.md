# 1. Audit Objective

This audit evaluates governance compliance within the repository according to
the Repository Audit Intelligence framework.

The objective is to determine whether engineering decisions are supported by:

- Defined governance rules.
- Documented standards.
- Decision traceability.
- Clear ownership.
- Controlled change processes.


---

# 2. Audit Scope

The evaluation covers:

- Engineering governance documentation.
- Policy adoption.
- Architecture decision records.
- Change management practices.
- Documentation ownership.
- Process consistency.


This audit does not evaluate:

- Legal compliance.
- Business governance.
- Financial controls.
- Organizational structure outside engineering.


---

# 3. Audit Methodology

The assessment follows the RAI governance evaluation process:

1. Review governance documents.
2. Analyze policy relationships.
3. Verify decision traceability.
4. Evaluate process maturity.
5. Document findings.


---

# 4. Governance Compliance Criteria

| Criterion | Description |
|---|---|
| Policy Definition | Engineering rules are formally documented |
| Decision Traceability | Technical decisions have documented history |
| Ownership Clarity | Responsibilities are defined |
| Change Control | Modifications follow controlled processes |
| Governance Consistency | Documents follow shared standards |

# 5. Findings


## RAI-005-F001

**Category:** Governance Documentation  
**Severity:** Medium  
**Status:** Resolved


### Finding

Engineering governance principles existed but required a formal compliance
evaluation layer.


### Impact

Without governance validation, standards may exist without measurable adoption.


### Resolution

Created governance compliance assessment as an official RAI audit dimension.

The evaluation now verifies:

- Policy existence.
- Document relationships.
- Decision traceability.
- Change management practices.


### Result

Governance principles have an established validation mechanism.


---

## RAI-005-F002

**Category:** Process Enforcement  
**Severity:** Medium  
**Status:** Monitoring


### Finding

Governance standards are documented, but automated enforcement mechanisms are
still under development.


### Impact

As repository complexity increases, manual compliance verification may become
less effective.


### Recommendation

Future improvements:

- Documentation linting.
- Policy validation scripts.
- Automated ADR checks.
- CI governance controls.


### Result

Governance maturity is established with future automation opportunities.
