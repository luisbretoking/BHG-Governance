# 1. Audit Objective

This audit evaluates compliance readiness within the repository according to
the Repository Audit Intelligence framework.

The objective is to determine whether the engineering system provides the
foundations required to support compliance through:

- Defined policies.
- Documented controls.
- Evidence preservation.
- Security practices.
- Governance processes.


---

# 2. Audit Scope

The evaluation covers:

- Compliance documentation principles.
- Internal policy alignment.
- Security and privacy considerations.
- Audit evidence capabilities.
- Governance controls.


This audit does not evaluate:

- Specific legal certifications.
- External regulatory approval.
- Jurisdiction-specific licensing.
- Formal compliance certification.


---

# 3. Audit Methodology

The assessment follows the RAI compliance evaluation process:

1. Review governance documentation.
2. Analyze control definitions.
3. Evaluate evidence availability.
4. Identify compliance gaps.
5. Document recommendations.


---

# 4. Compliance Readiness Criteria

| Criterion | Description |
|---|---|
| Policy Management | Rules and responsibilities are documented |
| Control Definition | Security and operational controls exist |
| Evidence Traceability | Decisions and changes are auditable |
| Privacy Awareness | Data protection principles are considered |
| Governance Alignment | Compliance integrates with engineering |

# 5. Findings


## RAI-017-F001

**Category:** Compliance Governance  
**Severity:** Medium  
**Status:** Resolved


### Finding

Compliance principles existed through security and governance documentation but
required a dedicated readiness evaluation.


### Impact

Without compliance validation, future systems may face:

- Missing evidence.
- Undefined controls.
- Difficulty demonstrating responsible operation.


### Resolution

Established compliance readiness as an official RAI assessment dimension.

The evaluation considers:

- Policies.
- Controls.
- Evidence.
- Governance alignment.


### Result

Compliance awareness is integrated into engineering maturity.


---

## RAI-017-F002

**Category:** Regulatory Preparation  
**Severity:** Medium  
**Status:** Monitoring


### Finding

Future products operating in regulated industries will require specialized
compliance frameworks.


### Impact

Fintech and identity systems may require additional controls depending on
jurisdiction and service scope.


### Recommendation

Future improvements:

- Privacy impact assessments.
- Regulatory requirement mapping.
- Compliance control registry.
- Audit evidence repository.
- Security certifications roadmap.


### Result

Compliance foundation exists with future specialization required.
