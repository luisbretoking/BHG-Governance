# Governance Adoption Model

**Document ID:** BHG-GOV-ADOPTION

**Version:** 1.0.0

**Status:** Active

---

# Purpose

This document defines how governance is adopted throughout the Breto's Holding Group ecosystem.

Every company, subsidiary, subholding or strategic initiative adopts the governance principles defined by BHG while preserving operational autonomy.

---

# Governance Philosophy

BHG governance is designed to scale.

It is not intended to centralize every operational decision.

Instead, it establishes a common constitutional framework that every organization inside the ecosystem shares.

---

# Constitutional Principle

One Constitution.

Multiple Organizations.

Independent Operations.

Shared Governance Principles.

---

# Governance Layers

Governance exists in multiple layers.

Layer 1

Breto's Holding Group Constitutional Governance.

Layer 2

Company Governance.

Layer 3

Business Unit Governance.

Layer 4

Project Governance.

Layer 5

Operational Procedures.

Every lower layer must remain compatible with every higher layer.

---

# Company Autonomy

Each company may create:

- constitutions
- policies
- standards
- engineering rules
- security rules
- AI rules
- operational manuals

without requesting authorization for every document.

However, those documents shall never violate BHG Constitutional Governance.

---

# Innovation Principle

Companies are encouraged to innovate.

Innovation is considered mandatory.

Governance must enable innovation instead of preventing it.

Whenever governance blocks innovation, a formal Governance Evolution Proposal should be submitted.

---

# Local Governance Councils

Every company may establish its own Governance Council.

Local councils govern local matters.

Only BHG Governance Council governs constitutional matters.

---

# Conflict Resolution

If conflicts arise:

Company Governance attempts resolution first.

If unresolved:

The issue escalates to BHG Governance Council.

Constitutional decisions are final.

---

# Shared Principles

Every organization shall preserve:

- traceability
- transparency
- version control
- documentation first
- human authority
- AI as advisor
- security by design
- evidence-based decisions

---

# Relationship with BEiA

BEiA assists every governance layer.

BEiA may:

- analyze
- simulate
- document
- review
- compare
- detect inconsistencies

BEiA never replaces human authority.

---

# Governance Expansion

Future companies automatically inherit this governance model.

No additional constitutional document is required unless explicitly approved.

---

# Final Principle

Governance should unify principles, not eliminate diversity.

Every company grows differently.

Every company remains aligned.

That balance is the foundation of Breto's Holding Group.
