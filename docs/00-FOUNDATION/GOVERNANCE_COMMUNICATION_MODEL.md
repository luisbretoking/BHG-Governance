# 1. Purpose

This document defines how governance information is communicated across the Breto's Holding Group ecosystem.

Governance communication shall always support transparency, accountability, traceability and institutional coherence.

---

# 2. Communication Principles

Governance communication shall always be:

- Clear
- Objective
- Timely
- Evidence-based
- Traceable
- Respectful
- Accessible

---

# 3. Official Communication Channels

Official governance communication may occur through:

- Governance Repository
- Approved Pull Requests
- Governance Decisions
- Governance Reports
- Governance Meeting Minutes
- Official Announcements

Unofficial communication shall never modify governance.

---

# 4. Communication Levels

Governance communication exists at five levels.

Level 1

Corporate Governance

Level 2

SubHolding Governance

Level 3

Company Governance

Level 4

Department Governance

Level 5

Project Governance

Each level communicates according to its scope.

---

# 5. Information Flow

Governance information may flow:

- Downward
- Upward
- Horizontally

Every flow shall preserve traceability.

---

# 6. Governance Proposals

Every governance proposal shall include:

- Purpose
- Scope
- Justification
- Expected Benefits
- Risks
- Impact Analysis
- Supporting Evidence

---

# 7. Governance Decisions

Official decisions shall communicate:

- Decision ID
- Responsible Authority
- Decision Date
- Effective Date
- Affected Documents
- Required Actions

---

# 8. Governance Notifications

Notifications inform.

They do not approve.

Notifications may communicate:

- New governance
- Version updates
- Deprecations
- Scheduled reviews
- Migration periods

---

# 9. Cross-Organization Communication

Companies shall share governance knowledge whenever beneficial.

Knowledge sharing shall never reduce organizational autonomy.

---

# 10. Role of BEiA

BEiA may:

- Draft reports
- Prepare summaries
- Organize evidence
- Generate comparisons
- Detect communication inconsistencies

BEiA never issues official governance communications.

Only authorized human authorities may do so.

---

# 11. Repository First

The governance repository is the official communication record.

Meetings, chats or emails are not considered authoritative unless reflected in the repository.

---

# 12. Transparency

Governance communication shall maximize transparency whenever legally possible.

Confidentiality shall be applied only when justified.

---

# 13. Communication Quality

Every governance communication shall be:

- Understandable by humans
- Machine-readable where possible
- Version controlled
- Searchable
- Referenced

---

# 14. Final Principle

Governance communication exists to reduce uncertainty, not increase bureaucracy.
