# ECOSYSTEM GOVERNANCE MODEL

Version: 1.0.0

Status: Official

Owner: Breto's Holding Group Governance Council

---

# Purpose

This document defines how governance is distributed across the Breto's Holding Group ecosystem.

The objective is to guarantee strategic alignment while preserving operational autonomy.

Every organization inside the ecosystem must understand:

- what it can decide,
- what it must inherit,
- what it may extend,
- what it may never contradict.

---

# Fundamental Principle

BHG governs principles.

Companies govern execution.

AI supports both.

---

# Governance Philosophy

The ecosystem follows a Federated Governance Model.

Authority flows from the Holding.

Execution flows from each organization.

Knowledge flows in every direction.

---

# Governance Layers

Layer 1

Breto's Holding Group

Defines:

- Constitution
- Vision
- Philosophy
- Governance Principles
- Corporate Standards
- Global Policies

Applies to every organization.

---

Layer 2

Subholdings

Example:

Ziva Latam

May create:

- internal governance
- engineering policies
- AI policies
- financial standards

Must remain compatible with BHG.

---

Layer 3

Companies

Each company manages:

operations

products

teams

engineering

commercial strategy

provided that no corporate principle is violated.

---

Layer 4

Projects

Projects inherit governance from the organization they belong to.

Projects never create corporate governance.

---

# Autonomy Principle

Each organization owns its decisions.

Autonomy is encouraged.

Isolation is forbidden.

Every organization remains part of one ecosystem.

---

# Inheritance Principle

Organizations inherit governance from their parent organization.

Inheritance is automatic unless explicitly overridden by an approved local policy.

---

# Override Principle

Local governance may extend corporate governance.

Local governance may specialize corporate governance.

Local governance may never contradict corporate governance.

---

# Local Innovation Principle

Organizations are encouraged to innovate.

Whenever corporate governance becomes an obstacle, a Governance Evolution Proposal shall be submitted.

Innovation is never blocked.

Innovation is reviewed.

---

# Shared Knowledge Principle

Knowledge generated by one organization should benefit the entire ecosystem whenever legally possible.

Knowledge sharing is the default.

Knowledge isolation is the exception.

---

# AI Principle

BEiA assists every governance layer.

BEiA never approves.

BEiA never governs.

BEiA never votes.

BEiA prepares evidence.

Humans decide.

---

# Conflict Resolution

Conflicts between organizations shall be escalated to the BHG Governance Council.

The Council issues the final interpretation.

---

# Long-Term Objective

Create one governance ecosystem capable of coordinating hundreds of autonomous organizations while remaining understandable, auditable and evolvable.
