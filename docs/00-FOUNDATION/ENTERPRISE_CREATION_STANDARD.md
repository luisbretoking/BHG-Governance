# Enterprise Creation Standard

# 1. Purpose

This standard defines the mandatory process for creating any new enterprise within the Breto's Holding Group (BHG) ecosystem.

Its objective is to ensure that every enterprise is created under a unified governance model, preventing inconsistencies, duplicated structures and undocumented organizations.

---

# 2. Scope

This standard applies to every:

- Company
- Subsidiary
- SubHolding
- Future legal entity
- Operational enterprise recognized by BHG

No enterprise may be considered official unless this standard has been completed.

---

# 3. Enterprise Creation Principles

Every enterprise shall be:

- Purpose-driven
- Governance-first
- Documentation-first
- Auditable
- Scalable
- Legally compliant
- Financially sustainable

---

# 4. Mandatory Creation Lifecycle

Every enterprise shall complete the following stages.

## Stage 1 — Strategic Need

Define:

- Business problem
- Opportunity
- Expected value
- Ecosystem impact

---

## Stage 2 — Governance Alignment

Verify compatibility with:

- Holding Constitution
- Governance Model
- Authority Model
- Enterprise Model

---

## Stage 3 — Organizational Definition

Define:

- Name
- Mission
- Vision
- Business scope
- Organizational type

---

## Stage 4 — Constitutional Documentation

Create:

- Company Constitution
- Governance documentation
- Organizational documentation

No operational work begins before documentation.

---

## Stage 5 — Approval

The Governance Council evaluates:

- Strategic alignment
- Risks
- Resource requirements
- Governance compatibility

Approval shall be documented.

---

## Stage 6 — Registration

The enterprise becomes officially recognized within BHG.

Required records include:

- Identifier
- Parent organization
- Creation date
- Responsible authority
- Version

---

## Stage 7 — Operational Activation

The enterprise may begin:

- Projects
- Product development
- Hiring
- Commercial operations

Only after governance approval.

---

# 5. Required Documentation

Every enterprise shall maintain:

- Constitution
- Organizational Model
- Governance Model
- Documentation Index
- Decision Register
- Risk Register
- Version History

---

# 6. Governance Requirements

Every enterprise shall:

- identify responsible authorities;
- define decision boundaries;
- document governance structure;
- define escalation paths;
- preserve traceability.

---

# 7. Documentation Requirements

All documentation shall follow Holding standards.

Documentation shall be:

- version-controlled;
- reviewable;
- searchable;
- auditable.

---

# 8. AI Requirements

AI may assist in:

- drafting documentation;
- risk analysis;
- architecture proposals;
- impact assessment;
- governance validation.

AI shall never approve the creation of an enterprise.

Only authorized human governance bodies may approve enterprise creation.

---

# 9. Minimum Deliverables

Before activation, every enterprise shall provide:

- Constitution
- Organizational structure
- Governance structure
- Initial roadmap
- Documentation repository
- Ownership definition
- Responsible authority

---

# 10. Evolution

An enterprise may evolve through:

- structural changes;
- business expansion;
- organizational growth;
- governance refinement.

Evolution shall preserve compatibility with the Holding governance architecture.

---

# 11. Compliance

Failure to comply with this standard may result in:

- delayed approval;
- governance review;
- operational restrictions;
- suspension of enterprise recognition.

---

# 12. Guiding Principle

Build deliberately.

Document completely.

Govern consistently.

Scale responsibly.
