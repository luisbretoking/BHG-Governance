# Company Constitution Template

## Purpose

This document defines the mandatory constitutional structure that every company within the Breto's Holding Group ecosystem shall adopt.

It provides a common governance architecture while allowing each company to define its own business identity, strategy and operational model.

The purpose is standardization, not centralization.

Each company remains autonomous within the limits established by the Holding Constitution.

---

# Constitutional Principles

Every company constitution shall preserve the following principles:

- Alignment with the Holding Constitution.
- Legal autonomy.
- Operational autonomy.
- Governance interoperability.
- Documentation First.
- Human decision supremacy.
- AI assistance only.
- Evidence-based governance.
- Complete traceability.
- Continuous evolution.

These principles cannot be removed.

Additional principles may be added when required.

# Mandatory Rules

Every Company Constitution shall:

- be written in English;
- use semantic versioning;
- remain compatible with the Holding Constitution;
- preserve document traceability;
- preserve historical versions;
- define governance authority;
- identify constitutional amendments.

---

# Prohibited Practices

A Company Constitution shall never:

- contradict the Holding Constitution;
- remove mandatory constitutional principles;
- transfer constitutional authority to AI;
- create undocumented governance bodies;
- redefine Holding authority.

---

# AI Participation

Artificial Intelligence may:

- draft documents;
- analyze impacts;
- detect conflicts;
- recommend improvements;
- simulate governance outcomes.

Artificial Intelligence shall never:

- approve constitutional changes;
- vote;
- replace governance councils;
- create binding decisions.

Human authority is always final.

---

# Constitutional Compatibility

Every Company Constitution shall explicitly identify:

- parent governance documents;
- applicable standards;
- applicable policies;
- inherited constitutional authority.

---

# Review Cycle

Recommended review frequency:

- Annual review.
- Extraordinary review after constitutional amendments.
- Extraordinary review after major organizational restructuring.

---

# Compliance

Every company joining the ecosystem shall adopt this constitutional structure before beginning operations.

No constitutional exceptions shall exist without explicit approval from the BHG Governance Council.
