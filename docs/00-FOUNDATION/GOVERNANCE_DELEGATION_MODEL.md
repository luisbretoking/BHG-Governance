# Governance Delegation Model

> Defines how governance authority is delegated, exercised, supervised and revoked throughout the Breto's Holding Group ecosystem.

---

# Purpose

This document establishes the official delegation model for governance authority within Breto's Holding Group.

Its objective is to enable decentralized governance while preserving constitutional integrity, accountability and institutional coherence.

---

# Delegation Principle

Authority may be delegated.

Accountability remains with the delegating authority unless explicitly transferred.

Constitutional authority is never delegated.

---

# Delegation Levels

Governance authority may be delegated across the following levels:

- Holding
- Subholding
- Company
- Business Unit
- Program
- Project
- Service
- Operational Team

Each level shall exercise only the authority explicitly granted.

---

# Delegable Authorities

Examples of delegable authority include:

- Operational governance
- Technical governance
- Engineering standards
- Project governance
- Product governance
- Service governance
- Internal procedures
- Local compliance activities

Delegation shall be documented.

---

# Non-Delegable Authorities

The following authorities shall remain exclusively under BHG Governance:

- Constitutional amendments
- Governance hierarchy
- Normative supremacy
- Corporate identity
- Authority model
- Governance principles
- Ecosystem-wide policies

These authorities cannot be transferred.

---

# Delegation Requirements

Every delegation shall identify:

- Delegating authority
- Receiving authority
- Scope
- Effective date
- Duration
- Responsibilities
- Limitations
- Review requirements

---

# Supervision

Delegated authority shall remain subject to governance oversight.

Supervision may include:

- Periodic reviews
- Compliance audits
- Performance evaluations
- Governance assessments

---

# Revocation

Delegated authority may be revoked when:

- Governance violations occur
- Constitutional conflicts arise
- Responsibilities are not fulfilled
- Organizational restructuring requires reassignment

Revocation shall preserve documentary traceability.

---

# Escalation

Issues exceeding delegated authority shall be escalated according to the Governance Escalation Model.

Delegated authorities shall not exceed their approved mandate.

---

# Accountability

Delegation does not eliminate accountability.

Every governance decision shall remain attributable to an identified governance authority.

---

# Governance Integrity

Delegation shall strengthen organizational agility without compromising governance consistency.

No delegation may create conflicting authority chains.

---

# Institutional Principle

> Authority may be delegated.

> Constitutional responsibility remains accountable.

> Delegation enables scale without sacrificing governance.
